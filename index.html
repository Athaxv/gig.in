How can I get a more affordable Home Insurance rate?

There are discounts offered by insurers that you can take advantage of to lower rates. They are:

  • When you bundle insurance policies (home, auto) with one insurer.
  • Increasing the deductible
  • Paying annually instead of monthly
  • Installing smoke detectors/monitored house alarms
  • Having never made an insurance claim
  • Being a member of a union, professional organization, or alumnus of certain universities
  • Being a senior citizen
  • Having a newly built home

Is it mandatory to have house insurance in Canada?

In Canada, home insurance isn’t mandatory. However, most banks and mortgage loaners will expect you have a house insurance policy as part of the financing agreement.

How much is house insurance in Canada monthly?

On a monthly basis, home insurance in Canada is determined based on your home’s location and its value as well as numerous other factors. It can vary depending on the location of your home, its age and condition, and other factors. See the question above for more details on how your insurance premium is calculated.

Are house insurance premiums going up in Canada?

Home insurance coverage options have generally increased by 5 percent as of 2022 in Canada. One of the primary reasons for this is extreme weather conditions due to climate change, causing more homes to take weather-related damages.

Does house insurance cost more if you have a mortgage?

Yes. The number of mortgages registered against the property is considered when defining your insurance premium, and there are savings for properties that are mortgage-free. Read the questions above for further details, talk to your broker, or get a home insurance quote today to get your house insurance premium.

Can I claim a broken fence on my homeowner's insurance?

It depends on your home insurance policy and other factors: the reasons why the fence was broken and if it is covered by an insured peril in your policy.
